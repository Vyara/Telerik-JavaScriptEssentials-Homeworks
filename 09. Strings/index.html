Example:

We are <mixcase>living</mixcase> in a <upcase>yellow submarine</upcase>. We <mixcase>don't</mixcase> have <lowcase>anything</lowcase> else. The expected result:
We are LiVinG in a YELLOW SUBMARINE. We dOn'T have anything else.
